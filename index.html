Document & Data Checklist Guide

Core Mandatory Documents

The following primary documents are required in digital format (PDF/Image) for all claim classifications:

  • Original Death Certificate: Issued by the Municipal Corporation / Registrar of Births and Deaths, confirming deceased details, date, and cause of death.
  • Cancelled Bank Cheque: Or passbook copy showing Nominee name, IFSC code, and Account number (used for direct transfer settlement).
  • Nominee Identity KYC: Aadhaar Card or PAN Card for identity biometric matching.

Conditional Documents (by Death Cause)

  • Accidental Death (Road crash, polytrauma, industrial accident):
    • First Information Report (FIR): Certified copy under Section 174 CrPC.
    • Post-Mortem Report (PMR): Certified autopsy findings showing accident shock/trauma.
  • Natural Death (Illness, disease):
    • Hospital Case Records: Discharge summary, treatment logs (mandatory for early claims within 3 years of policy commencement).

Required Intake Data Points

  • Policy Details: Policy Number, Commencement Date, Premium history.
  • Beneficiary Details: Nominee Name, Aadhaar number.
  • Claim Parameters: Cause of death, place of death, date of death.
